Zhang Hao

The first foreign center in K-pop

Here's a list of fun facts about the rising "5th gen it boy":


  • Born: Zhang Hao was born in Nanping, Fujian, China on 25th July, 2000. His zodiac sign is Leo.
  • Trainee Period: His trainee period lasted for about 1 year in YueHua Entertainment, the lowest duration among all his bandmates in K-pop group, Zerobaseone.
  • Talents: He plays the violin and is fluent in Mandarin, Korean, and English.
  • Favorites: His favorite K-pop groups include GOT7 and Tomorrow X Together.

His recent achievements include:


  • 2016: Zhang Hao took the Gaokao (NCEE) exam at the age of 16 and got accepted into China University of Geosciences, which ranks #5 in the world for this discipline. Normally, the exam is taken at the age of 18.
  • 2018: He realized his true passion was music and began learning the violin. Within 6 months, he took CSATs for Arts and placed 1st in the Fujian Province. Entered the Fujian Normal University as a Music Education major. The music department at the Fujian Normal Uni is #1 in China.
  • 2019: He got his teacher's license to teach elementary and high school students alongside his university studies.
  • 2019-2021: He played the violin at weddings alongside studies to save money for moving to South Korea.
  • 2022: Joined the MNet survival show, Boys Planet, and got chosen as the G-Group Center.
  • Jan 2023: Got 4 stars, the maximum amount, in both the Star Level Test as well as the re-evaluation.
  • Feb 2023: Was selected as the leader for Kill This Love G-Group and had the highest score in his team. His team was also the only one from G-Group to win in that round. Additionally, he reached #7 in the overall rankings.
  • March 2023: Reached #5 of the overall rankings and won the highest score in Tomboy team, as well as the highest points awarded in Mnet survival show history, giving him an additional point benefit overall. He also earned the award of getting to perform Tomboy on MCountDown.
  • April 2023: Got the highest score, killing (main) part as well as the leader position for Over Me, and the team stood first overall again, with Zhang Hao maintaining his streak as #1 in all performances. He also reached #2 in overall rankings.
  • Finale - April 2023: Bagged the fan-voted killing (main) part of Jelly Pop, as well as #1 in the overall rankings, making him the first foreign center in K-pop history. He was the only contestant to never drop in rankings and debut.
